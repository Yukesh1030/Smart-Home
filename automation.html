What is Automation?

The Symphony of a Connected Household

Home automation is more than just remote controls. It's the ability to link multiple physical devices together and set conditional actions based on schedule, sensors, or presence.

With STACKLY, you write rules that describe how your home behaves under different situations. For example: "If the front door is opened AND it is after sunset, turn on hallway lights and play lounge music."

Custom Modes

Morning, Night, and Away Modes

We configure three core automated modes out of the box to streamline your typical daily schedules.

Morning Mode

Blinds slowly raise, temperature warms up, bathroom geysers switch on, and coffee machine pre-heats at 7:00 AM.

Triggered at Sunrise

Night Mode

Doors lock automatically, security cameras arm, lighting dims to 10% sleep glow, and all heavy media outlets power down.

Triggered at 11:00 PM

Away Mode

All lights switch off, HVAC enters low-energy eco mode, motion sensors arm, and instant push notification stream activates.

Triggered on Presence Exit
