FAQ Help Center

Common Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about STACKLY features, installation, and assistant compatibility.

Do STACKLY switches require rewiring my home?

No. STACKLY smart switches are designed to fit directly into your existing back boxes. They use your current wiring infrastructure, requiring only standard neutral and phase connections. No walls are broken, and installation takes under 15 minutes per switch plate.

Will my devices still work if my internet goes down?

Yes. STACKLY controllers establish a local area network (LAN) using your home router. Basic physical triggers, schedules, and custom automation routines run locally without requiring cloud servers, so your lights and schedules work perfectly even without internet connection.

Can I share controls with my family members?

Yes. The STACKLY application features a "Home Sharing" setting. Invite family members or guests using their email addresses. You can grant full administrator permissions or restrict permissions to specific rooms, devices, or timetables.

How much energy can I save using STACKLY?

Homeowners save an average of 15% to 22% on monthly energy bills. Savings are achieved through automated standby cut-offs, smart scheduling of water heaters during off-peak hours, and motion-based lighting controls.
